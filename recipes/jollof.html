Jollof-Rice

Jollof-Rice is a west-african rice dish

It is a fairly simple dish. You simply cook rice in a stew.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Onions
  • Rice
  • Some Oil
  1. Heat the oil in a pan
  2. Put the diced onions in the oil and fry them
  3. Include the tomato paste
  4. Afterwards iclude the rice
